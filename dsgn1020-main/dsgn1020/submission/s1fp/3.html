Black Coffee

Teh

MILK

It gets a little complicated here. "Kopi/Teh" alone stands for condensed milk, the suffix "O" is pure black coffee, whereas "C" uses evaporated milk.

Kopi O
/ko.pi.o/

No Milk

Kopi
/ko.pi/

Condensed Milk

Kopi C
/ko.pi.see/

Evaporated Milk

SUGAR

Choose your sugar level, or hold your peace forever.

Kosong
/go.song/

No Sugar

Xiudai
/sew.dai/

Less Sugar

Gadai
/ka.dai/

More Sugar
